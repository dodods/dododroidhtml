Ya Badrotim

Ya badrotim Min Haza kulla kamali
Maza yu’ab, Yu’abiru ‘an ‘ulaka maqoli
Antalladzi asyroqta fi ufuqil ‘ula
Famahauta bilanwari kulla dholali

Ya badrotim, Min Haza kulla kamali
Maza yu’ab, Yu’abiru ‘an ‘ulaka maqoli

Binnuri wal in’ami wal ifdholi

Ya badrotim Min Haza kulla kamali

Abada ma’al ibkari wal asoli
